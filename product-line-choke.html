Product Description

Line chokes are used to provide improved protection against overvoltages on the line supply and to reduce harmonic distortion of the current produced by the drive.

Key Features

  • Harmonic Mitigation: Significantly reduces harmonic distortion (THD).
  • Protection Features: Absorbs power line spikes and protects against transient overvoltage.
  • Drive Performance: Reduces nuisance tripping and extends the life of VFDs.
  • Efficiency: Improves the true power factor of the system.

Advantages

  • Enhances reliability of motor and drive systems.
  • Cost-effective solution for improving power quality.
  • Compact and easy to integrate into existing panels.

Applications

Line Chokes are essential for a wide range of motor control applications, including those involving Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs), DC Drives, and Soft Starters.

Industries Using This Product

HVAC, Water & Wastewater Treatment, Industrial Automation, Renewable Energy (Wind Mills, Solar Pumping), and any industry utilizing AC motor drives.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I use a line choke?

A line choke is recommended for all VFD installations to improve power quality, reduce harmonics, and protect the drive from line disturbances. It is especially important in installations with poor power quality or multiple drives on the same line.

What is the difference between 3% and 5% impedance?

A 5% impedance choke provides better harmonic mitigation compared to a 3% choke but will cause a slightly higher voltage drop. The choice depends on the specific requirements of the application and the existing power quality.
