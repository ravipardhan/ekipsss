Product Description

As a premier isolation transformer supplier based in Gujarat, we design units that provide complete galvanic isolation between input and output AC power. This physical separation, created by an electrostatic shield, ensures the output circuit is protected from electrical noise, spikes, and grounding issues, making them vital for sensitive equipment.

Key Features

  • High noise attenuation
  • Galvanic isolation
  • Electrostatic shielding
  • Protection against voltage spikes

Applications

Medical equipment, CNC machines, Sensitive laboratory instruments, Audio/Video recording studios, IT & Networking server rooms.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main purpose of an isolation transformer?

It is primarily used to transfer electrical power from a source of alternating current (AC) power to some equipment or device while isolating the powered device from the power source, usually for safety reasons.

Does it reduce electrical noise?

Yes, due to the electrostatic shield between the primary and secondary windings, it significantly reduces common-mode noise.
