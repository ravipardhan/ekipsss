Our Core Offerings

Professional transformer solutions tailored for your industry.

Control Transformers

A transformer that is used to step down the voltage to power the control devices of a circuit or machine. The reduced voltage provides a much safer environment for technicians working on the equipment.

Enquire Now

Line Chokes

Line chokes are used to provide improved protection against overvoltages on the line supply and to reduce harmonic distortion of the current produced by the drive.

Enquire Now

Three Phase Transformers

Three-phase transformers are passive machines that pass electrical energy between circuits. In the secondary circuit, a magnetic flux induces an electromotive force (emf).

Enquire Now

Buck Boost Transformers

A buck-boost transformer works by taking input voltage from the primary side of the transformer and stepping it up or down – depending on the preferred configuration.

Enquire Now

Lift UPS Transformers

These ranges are with multi secondary type which feeds for Door closure controls, DC Braking, SMPS module, Illumination, ARD (Auto Rescue Device) which operates with high frequency & 3 phase low voltage mode.

Enquire Now

UPS and Ard Transformers

The power flows via the rectifier, inverter and transformer to the output, with the transformer used to step up the AC voltage levels, protect the UPS from load disruptions and provide galvanic isolation. Transformerless UPS operate in the same way, apart from one key difference.

Enquire Now

Sine Wave Transformers

An inverter is an electronic device that converts direct current (DC) to alternating current (AC). A sine wave is a continuous wave that describes a smooth repetitive oscillation.

Enquire Now

Inverter Transformers

In power conversion applications an inverter is an electronic transformer which converts power from a Direct Current (D.C) source into Alternating Current (A.C) power.

Enquire Now

Auto Transformers

An autotransformer is an electrical transformer with only one winding. The "auto" prefix refers to the single coil acting alone, not to any kind of automatic mechanism.

Enquire Now

Isolation Transformer

Ensures complete galvanic isolation for noise reduction and safety, protecting sensitive electronic equipment from voltage spikes and line disturbances.
