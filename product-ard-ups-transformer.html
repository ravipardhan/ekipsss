Critical power components for Auto Rescue Devices and Uninterruptible Power Supplies.

Product Description

The power flows via the rectifier, inverter and transformer to the output, with the transformer used to step up the AC voltage levels, protect the UPS from load disruptions and provide galvanic isolation. Transformerless UPS operate in the same way, apart from one key difference.

Key Features

  • Optimized for battery operation
  • High efficiency at partial loads
  • Compact design for panel mounting
  • Thermal protection included

Applications

Elevator Auto Rescue Devices, Online/Offline UPS, Emergency Lighting Systems, Security Systems.
