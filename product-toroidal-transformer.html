Toroidal Transformer

Superior efficiency and low magnetic interference in a compact donut shape.

Product Description

Toroidal transformers use a ring-shaped core which allows for a smaller, lighter, and more efficient design compared to traditional EI core transformers. They emit very low magnetic fields, making them ideal for sensitive audio and medical equipment.

Key Features

  • 50% lighter than standard transformers
  • Low hum and noise
  • High efficiency (up to 95%)
  • Easy single-bolt mounting

Applications

Audio Amplifiers, Medical Devices, Power Supplies, Lighting Systems, Stabilizers.
