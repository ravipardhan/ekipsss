Lift UPS 3 Phase Transformer

Reliable backup power integration for elevators and escalators.

Get a Quote

Product Description

These ranges are with multi secondary type which feeds for Door closure controls, DC Braking, SMPS module, Illumination, ARD (Auto Rescue Device) which operates with high frequency & 3 phase low voltage mode.

Key Features

  • High surge capability
  • Compact footprint
  • Low noise operation
  • Compatible with regenerative drives

Applications

Passenger elevators, Freight lifts, Escalators, Moving walkways.
