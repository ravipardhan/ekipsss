Our Product Expertise

Providing solutions for both standard and custom design transformers.

Control Transformers

Used to step down voltage to power control devices, providing a safer environment for technicians. Ideal for HVAC, relays, and contactors.

Line Chokes

Provide protection against overvoltages and reduce harmonic distortion of the current produced by drives.

Three Phase Transformers

Passive machines that transfer electrical energy between circuits, stepping voltage up or down without altering frequency.

Buck Boost Transformers

Steps input voltage up or down to provide rectified voltage for connected devices or machines.

Lift UPS Transformers

Multi-secondary type feeding door controls, braking, and ARD, operating with high frequency & 3-phase low voltage mode.

UPS and ARD Transformers

Step up AC voltage levels, protect UPS from load disruptions, and provide galvanic isolation.

Sine Wave Transformers

Associated with inverters to convert DC to AC, producing a smooth repetitive oscillation similar to conventional power.

Inverter Transformers

Converts power from a DC source into AC power, used in voltage-fed and current-fed inverter applications.

Auto Transformers

Transformers with a single winding acting as both primary and secondary, offering a compact voltage conversion solution.
