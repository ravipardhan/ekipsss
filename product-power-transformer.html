Product Description

Our Power Transformers are the backbone of electrical grids, designed to handle high voltages for efficient power transmission over long distances. They are engineered for maximum reliability, durability, and performance under heavy load conditions.

Key Features

  • High Efficiency: Built with low-loss CRGO cores and optimized winding to minimize energy losses.
  • Robust Construction: Designed to withstand electrical and mechanical stresses during operation and short circuits.
  • Advanced Cooling: Equipped with ONAN/ONAF/OFAF cooling systems for effective thermal management.
  • Safety Features: Includes Buchholz relay, PRV, and oil temperature indicators for safe operation.

Applications

Power generation plants (thermal, hydro, nuclear), transmission substations, and large-scale industrial complexes.
